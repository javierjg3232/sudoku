## Description
Sudoku, without the noise.

A calm place to think in numbers: a warm, minimal board, one accent color,
and nothing competing for your attention. Every puzzle is generated with a
single guaranteed solution — no guessing required, just logic.

FOUR DIFFICULTIES
From gentle Easy grids to Expert boards that earn their name. The generator
verifies a unique solution every time.

A NEW PUZZLE EVERY DAY
The Daily Puzzle is the same board for everyone, every day. Keep your streak
alive and compare times.

TOOLS THAT RESPECT THE PUZZLE
• Pencil notes with one-tap auto-fill for the selected cell
• Three hints per game — enough to get unstuck, not enough to spoil it
• Undo, smart highlighting, and conflict detection as you play
• Optional 3-mistake limit for purists

MADE TO BE LIVED IN
• Warm light theme and a true dark mode
• Times, win rates, and streaks tracked per difficulty
• Auto-saves the moment you leave; resume exactly where you were
• Subtle haptics, a quiet timer, and a little confetti when you earn it

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
No account. No ads. No tracking. Your games never leave your device.
